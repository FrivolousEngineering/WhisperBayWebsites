Our shop
We are serving the community!

We are an independent village store (shop) offering a wide range of services for the local and wider community. Including a same day personal shopper service.

We have a huge range of produce and household goods, so whether you need to do a big shop or just top up, we have pretty much everything you need.

If we don't stock a particular item, just ask and we will see what we can do. With our regular deliveries from our network of local suppliers, we always have a good variety of fresh and frozen food available. You can also find some more unusual and intriguing ingredients from local specialist suppliers.

Cake Galore. Our lovely cakerey bakes everyday so we have a choice of homemade cakes.

We have deliveries of fruit & veg daily so stay healthy and look out for home grown produce from our area.

Our wooden dresser is a delightful choice of tipples with many Cornish Ciders and Beers. We like the theory Grown local, Shop local and Sip local!
