*** Welcome to the Best Playground Toys Page! ***

Best Playground Toys!!! They are watching...

Slides

Slides are the best! You can climb up and slide down super fast. Be careful, though, sometimes when you reach the bottom, you might feel like someone's watching... They say if you look closely, you can see a shadow that wasn't there before.

Swings

Swings let you fly through the air! Pump your legs to go higher and higher. Just don't swing too high, or you might see the shadows moving... Some say there's a ghost that swings with you at night.

Monkey Bars

Monkey bars are great for climbing and hanging around. Sometimes you can almost hear whispers when you're upside down... Legend has it, a child once fell and now haunts the monkey bars.

Seesaw

The seesaw is fun with a friend. You can go up and down together. Just make sure you have a real friend with you, not the one that only appears at night... Some say the ghost of a lonely child seeks a friend on the seesaw.

Hope you enjoy the best toys! Look behind you...
