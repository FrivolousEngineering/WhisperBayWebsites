About Us

Welcome to our little green corner of Whisper Bay! Our community garden has been a hub for gardening enthusiasts, nature lovers, and families since 1964. Located amidst the serene landscapes of our beautifull Whisper Bay, it's more than just a place to grow veggies—it's where friendships bloom and green thumbs unite.


We strive to foster a sense of community and promote sustainable gardening practices. It doesn't matter if you're old or new to the hobby, as long as you want to get your hands dirty, you've come to the right place!
