Crystals and Their Properties

Clear Quartz: Known as the "master healer," clear quartz is said to amplify intentions, clarify thoughts, and bring balance to the body.

Amethyst: This powerful stone is believed to promote emotional calmness, clarity, and spiritual connection. It's also said to help overcome addiction and promote a restful sleep.

Rose Quartz: Associated with the heart chakra, rose quartz is thought to foster unconditional love, self-love, and compassion. It's also said to soothe emotional wounds and promote inner peace.

Citrine: This vibrant stone is believed to stimulate creativity, positivity, and abundance. It's also said to help manifest prosperity and improve relationships.

Lapis Lazuli: Known for its deep blue color, lapis lazuli is thought to enhance intuition, psychic abilities, and spiritual connection. It's also said to promote wisdom, honesty, and self-awareness.
