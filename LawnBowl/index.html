Whisper Bay Bowling Club
Home Page
A bowls club for all tastes, the club is currently the most successful in the county as far as competitions go but we are much more than that. We have 3 teams in the league, one in each division. We have dedicated players who enjoy a roll up (even in the winter on our all weather green!) There are good fixtures a plenty to take you to all parts. Add to this thriving snooker, table tennis and short mat teams in the winter and there is something for all. Quizzes, race nights and skittles complete the offer at what is the Bowls Club of the Year 1990!
