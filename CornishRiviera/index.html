About The Cornish Riviera Project

The Cornish Riviera Project is an exciting new venture led by Henry Kempthorne, aiming to transform the land that used the old tin mine into a premier holiday destination. Our vision is to create a luxurious holiday park that offers top-notch amenities and attractions, drawing tourists from all over to experience the beauty of Whisper Bay.

Project Details

The holiday park will feature:

  • Luxury accommodations
  • World-class dining
  • Outdoor adventure activities
  • Entertainment facilities
  • Wellness and spa services
