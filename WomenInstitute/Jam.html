Jam Recipes

Strawberry Jam

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg fresh strawberries
  • 800 g granulated sugar
  • 1 lemon (juice and zest)

Instructions:

  1. Hull and halve the strawberries.
  2. In a large pot, combine strawberries, sugar, and lemon juice and zest.
  3. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium-high heat, stirring frequently.
  4. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until thickened, about 20-30 minutes.
  5. Test for doneness by placing a small amount on a cold plate; if it wrinkles when pushed, it's ready.
  6. Remove from heat and let cool slightly before transferring to sterilized jars.
  7. Seal and store in a cool, dark place.
