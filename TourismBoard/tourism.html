Local Attractions

Explore the stunning beaches, historic lighthouse, and the remnants of our once-thriving tin mine. Whisper Bay offers a unique blend of natural beauty and rich history.

Accommodations

Stay at one of our cozy bed and breakfasts, charming cottages, or luxurious hotels. Whether you're looking for a rustic retreat or a comfortable stay, Whisper Bay has something for everyone.

Restaurants

Enjoy delicious local cuisine at our variety of restaurants and cafes. From fresh seafood to traditional Cornish pasties, there's something to satisfy every palate.

Summer Fête

Join us for the annual Summer Fête, a weekend of fun activities, live music, and local crafts. It's the perfect way to experience the community spirit of Whisper Bay.
